Problem
Established electrician over-reliant on branded searches. No visibility for high-intent commercial service queries across Milwaukee metro.
Solution
Content silo implementation (Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC), Core Web Vitals overhaul, local business schema, and "near me" query optimization.
Results — GSC Verified
Impressions grew 83.3K → 171K (+105.3%). Clicks grew 1.69K → 1.86K (+10%). Site now ranks for thousands of high-volume commercial service keywords it never appeared for before. Brand's digital footprint doubled.

🧠 Strategist's Analysis
Why Avg Position shifted 15.8→20.4: By entering high-volume terms like "electrician near me", we rank initially at positions 15–30 — mathematically lowering the average while doubling total brand visibility.
Why CTR dropped 2%→1.1%: Discovery traffic always has lower CTR than branded. But 1.1% of 171K = 1,881 clicks vs 2% of 83K = 1,660. We get more absolute clicks reaching entirely new audiences.
